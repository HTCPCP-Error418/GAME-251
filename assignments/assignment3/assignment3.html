Assignment Prompt Summary

Create a game played on the surface of a Rubik's Cube. Assume that there are magnetic pawns, representing the players, which can stick to the sides and bottom of the cube. You don't have to actually build a complete game, but experiment with the documentation needed to explain to others how your puzzle should be completed, then:

  1. Write approximately 500 words to explain your game through a form of game documentation to explain this game you design to others
  2. Add at least one image/graphic that you could include in the documentation.
