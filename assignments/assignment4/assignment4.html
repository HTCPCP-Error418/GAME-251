Assignment Prompt Summary

Design a game using exactly one of each of the following mechanisms of chance:

  • A shuffled deck of cards
  • Tiles delt and flipped
  • Tiles blind-drawn from a bag
Complete the following steps:
  1. Create a 1-2 sentence explanation (AKA "executive summary") of your game.
  2. What is your interpretation of the term "game concept," and describe the concept of your game.
  3. Is there a game that this is based on, or how did you think of this idea? Did you do any research to prepare the content?
  4. Describe the visuals of your game; what would it look like? Attach one visual of the game.
  5. What is your interpretation of the term "game play," and describe the game play of your game in 2-3 sentences.
  6. Create directions/documentation for your game of at least 200 words.
